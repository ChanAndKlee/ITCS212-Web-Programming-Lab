A Day Lost In Japan

ᐢ⑅ᐢ About ᐢ⑅ᐢ

Japan is the origin of manga and anime, in this trip, we will take you to the place where is the locations within the 'Kimetsu no Yaiba' or 'Demon Slayer' in English. This movie received the AFA Academy Highest-Grossing Asian Film of 2020, therefore people who love watching anime definitely should watch this once in life. The goal of the trip is to learn the history of their culture and visit the nature place.
